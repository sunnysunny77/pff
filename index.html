COMMUNITY FOOTBALL

Get involved by joining your local Community Football Club as a player, coach, administrator or volunteer. As many as 29 Community Football Leagues and 272 Clubs exist throughout regional and metro WA for more than 50,000 men, women, boys and girls. The introduction of a football council is the perfect example of PFF supporting grassroots footy.

PHYSICAL DISTANCE REQUIREMENTS

By limiting spectator numbers, currently to the 1 person per 2 square metre rule, social distancing at venues can be maintained. Club officials will be present as Covid Marshals and will be ensuring patrons are complying with social distancing. Grandstand seating will also be marked.
