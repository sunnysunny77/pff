COMMUNITY MEMBERS

PFF is supporting league footy. Get involved by joining your local Community Football Club as a player, coach, administrator or member. As many as 29 Community Football Leagues and 272 Clubs exist throughout regional and metro WA for more than 50,000 men, women, boys and girls. Please contact us or send us a membership enquiry to get the ball rolling.

FIXTURE PHYSICAL DISTANCING

By limiting spectator numbers, currently to the 1 person per 2 square metre rule, social distancing at league bracket games can be maintained. Club officials will be present as Covid Marshals and will be ensuring patrons are complying with social distancing. Grandstand seating will also be marked.
